Khalil Gibran School

A state of emergency was announced in Morocco on Saturday, April 11th, which required the complete shut down of schools all over the country. Therefore, a classmate and I have decided to create a platform on "Discord" for online learning and present it to the school's Director to continue studying on Monday without losing valuable class sessions. We have completed the platform within a few hours, and I have contacted the Director for a personal meeting to express my concerns and introduce our solution. The school's administration then accepted the plan, and our platform was put in place to replace the traditional face to face classes. We were the only school in the region to face the problem with such precision and without shutting down for a week.

I have then gathered a group of friends, whom I have given a short demo on how to use the platform so that each one of them could provide a presentation to another group of students and professors, and so forth.

Finally, I was also in charge of creating and providing Class schedules for the students, keeping professors logs, reporting to the administration, adjusting the platform to address complaints, and giving weekly tutorials to new students and professors.

The platfom today is always operational and is being used as an alumnus server for Class of 2020.
